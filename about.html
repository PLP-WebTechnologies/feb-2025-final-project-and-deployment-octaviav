About Make a Difference

We are a passionate organization dedicated to creating meaningful change in our communities through innovative programs and compassionate support.

Our Story

How a simple idea transformed into a movement of hope and change.

A Simple Beginning

Make a Difference was born in 2010 when our founder, Sarah Johnson, witnessed the struggles of underserved communities in her hometown. What began as a small weekend food drive quickly blossomed into something much larger as more volunteers joined the cause.

Throughout our journey, we've remained committed to our core belief: that small actions, when multiplied by compassionate people, can transform communities. From our humble beginnings with just 5 volunteers, we've grown to a network of over 1,000 changemakers across the country.

Today, Make a Difference operates multiple programs focused on education, healthcare access, environmental sustainability, and community development. While we've grown substantially, our approach remains personal and community-centered, ensuring that every individual we work with feels valued and empowered.
